Step #1

1. 
Click on "New Eblast" at the top left of the screen
2.
Click on "Begin" in step #1 Set up your eblast
  • Select the desired subscriber list for your eblast and fill in the required fields. Then click on "Submit".

Step #2

3.
Go to step # 2 Design your content
4.
Select your file (JPEG or HTML) or select a template to create your ad
  • You can add a web link on each image, if desired
  • Click on "Submit"

Step #3

5.
Go to step # 3 Schedule
  • Select the different dates and times for this eblast. One or more dates can be chosen for the same Quick Presse.

Step #4

6.
Lastly, you must preview your Quick Presse before confirming the sending. Simply press "Preview" at the top left. You can see it from a browser or do a test in the email related to the account. If everything is good to go, simply activate the request by selecting YES.
7.
